Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian dish made with eggs, hard cheese, and black pepper.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta or Guanciale
  • Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Fry the pork until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese.
  4. Combine all ingredients with the hot pasta.

Simple Roasted Chicken

Perfectly seasoned and roasted chicken that's tender and juicy.

Ingredients:

  • Whole chicken
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Herbs (rosemary, thyme)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Rub chicken with oil, salt, pepper, and herbs.
  3. Roast for about 1.5 hours, or until cooked through.
